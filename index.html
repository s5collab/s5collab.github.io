Collaboration and Members

The S5 Collaboration consists of roughly 30 members spread accross many institutions. S5 is a collaboration between the members DES Milky Way Working Group and a group of Australian astronomers. S5 leadership team helps to coordination the operational and scientific efforts of the collaboration.
